home inspiration top 5's

zhu tiexiong

Zhu Tiexiong's work uses amazing special effects and story lines to promote traditional culture. The expression of emotion and the spread of positive energy in his work inspired me to explore.

niu yifei

Niu Yifei's works are skillfully created with natural materials (leaves, stones, etc.). His works are a close link between nature and art. His way of creating has given me a new sense of imagination for design.

liu xiaolingtong

Lix Xiaolingtong is famous for his role as the Wukong (Monkey King). The internet says it took him 17 years to complete the character of Sun Wukong. This may be the reason why among all the versions of Journey to the West, only his performance of the Monkey King will become a classic character in the minds of a generation. His dedication and persistence to his art made me realize that good creations require in-depth knowledge and time investment.
